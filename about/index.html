About Us

We love technology and helping our clients find technology that facilitates success.

Provide cost-effective services and solutions for growing businesses. Our Mission

Peak Solutions is a web development company headerquartered in Harrisonburg, Virginia. The Shenandoah Valley and surrounding Charlottesville area provide great support for technology companies and great people to serve. Though we take great pride in serving our fellow Virginia businesses, we also provide services outside of the state.

In addition to small businesses and corporations, we take great pleasure in providing technology solutions for non-profit organizations. These organizations need valuable technology in order to find donors and community support. Many online services offer extra features to non-profit organiztions at reduced or no extra charge, so we make sure to ask about non-profit status during our initial conversations.

Technology is very affordable and it most certainly should not leave you feeling disappointed with your investment. If you have been confused or dissatisfied with other technology firms, then drop us a line and we will try to help you find the right solutions.

Our Process

  • Define

    Before recommending services, we perform a simple interview to find out your current business needs. After accurately defining your needs, we provide recommendations and your choices result in a project plan. We explicitly document what we deliver and support, so there are no surprises.

  • Develop

    We convert the project plan into sprint tasks and execute in an agile environment. Some services may require your feedback at various milestones or sprints, such as web design.

  • Deliver

    Delivering a completed service is a rewarding experience for us and our customers. The final project meeting recaps the deliverables and address any issues.
